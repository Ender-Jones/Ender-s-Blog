## 2026-07-01
<!-- energy: 2 -->
### WIP
- Full codebase audit.

### Done
- The audit overturned "the experiments are done". A library version mismatch had produced a cluster of silent failures: the first stage's pretrained weights were never actually loaded into the second stage, two model parameters had been silently inert for months, and early stopping was reading the outer validation split — an optimistic bias.
- Two of the four physiological features were also near-useless as computed: over-detection at the sampling rate, and a within-window normalisation that erased the absolute level.
- The data pipeline is leakage-safe. Triple-checked. That part holds.
- Fixed all of it the same day and defined the new protocol.

### Decision
- Every earlier result is void. Full rerun, no exceptions — the honest numbers will be lower and that is fine.
- Fixes: real weight loading, inner-validation early stopping, literature-grounded features, normalisation removed where it destroyed signal.

### Risk
- Weeks of compute to redo, with the journal deadline already moved once.

### Next
- Package everything for the training box and rerun.

### Public
- The audit voided every old result. Rerunning everything — honest numbers only.
